Introduction

Parlance is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • Game Data: All your game progress, learnt words, and statistics are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Leaderboard (if enabled): If you submit your score to a leaderboard, only your chosen display name and score are sent. No other personal data is collected.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience.
  • To display your progress and statistics locally.
  • To show leaderboard rankings (if enabled).

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies in the Parlance app.

Third-Party Services

Parlance may use third-party dictionary APIs to provide word definitions. These services may collect anonymous usage data as described in their own privacy policies. We do not control or have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

Parlance does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require account creation.

Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
