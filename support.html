How to Play

Swipe right (→) if you think the word definition is correct, or swipe left (←) if you think it's incorrect. The game ends when you make a wrong swipe!

Features

  • Word Learning: Every word you encounter is automatically saved to your "Learnt Words" collection
  • Dictionary: Tap on words to see detailed definitions and pronunciations
  • Statistics: Track your progress with correct/incorrect counts and streaks
  • Offline Play: The game works completely offline

Tips for Success

  • Take your time to read the definition carefully
  • Look for context clues in the definition
  • Consider multiple meanings of words
  • Review your learnt words regularly to reinforce your knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the words selected?

Words are carefully curated from various difficulty levels to provide an engaging learning experience.

Can I play offline?

Yes! The game works completely offline, including all word definitions and pronunciations.

How do I clear my learnt words?

Open the "Learnt Words" section and tap "Clear All Words" to reset your collection.
